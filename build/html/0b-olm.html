Showing your progress

Showing your progress#

As you work through EarlyAccess, you can track your progress on the side navigation menu. There are two methods for this:

  • Confidence self-ratings (from 1-5)

  • Short question progress

See the menu on the left for a key for both of these.

EarlyAccess contains 17 multiple-choice questions as well as 8 multiple-choice questions in a final quiz. Your short question progress will also be shown for subheadings within a page on the sub-menu on the right.
